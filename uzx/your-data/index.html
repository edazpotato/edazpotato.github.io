How we use your data

What we store

  • Your Discord User ID.
  • Your Discord Guild (Server) ID.
  • Any information you share with us such as language or colour preferences.
We store Your Discord User ID in order to provide you with a customized experience using our application.
We store Your Discord Guild (AKA Server) ID in order to provide members of your Guild (AKA Server) with a customized experience using our application.
We store information that you share with us in order to provide services including language localization, customized Embed (A Discord feature) colours and customized Bot (The UZX Discord Chatbot Application) prefixes.
