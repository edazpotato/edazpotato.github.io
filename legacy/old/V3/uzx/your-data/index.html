UZX Data Policy

When we gather your data

We gather your data when:

  • You log into the UZX Web Dashboard
  • You change a setting in the UZX Web Dashboard
  • You interact with the UZX Discord ChatBot

How we gather your data

We gather your data via:

  • Commands that you use when interacting with our Discord ChatBot
  • The Discord API when you sign into the UZX Web Dashboard
  • Input that you submit to the UZX Web Dashboard

Why we gather your data

We gather your data in order to provide you with a customized experience while using our services. Examples of customized features include:

  • Language selection
  • Customized Guild (AKA Server) ChatBot command prefixes

How long we store your data

We will store your data indefinitely or until you request it's deletion.

Where we store your your data

We store your data in our main database, which is hosted on a secure VPS which is secured via passwords and two factor authentication.

How we store your data

We store your data in a secure database.

Why we store your data

We store your data in order to provide persistent features such as:

  • Language selection
  • Customized Guild (AKA Server) ChatBot command prefixes

How to request deletion of your data

If for any reason you want your data deleted from our databases just send us an email at edazpotato@gmail.com detailing the data that you want deleted and proof you have permission to request the deletion of that data.
